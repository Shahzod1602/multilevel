## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
Multilevel Speaking is your AI-powered coach for the Uzbekistan Multilevel (B1/B2/C1) English Speaking exam. Practice exactly the test format with instant AI scoring on every answer.

🎯 WHY MULTILEVEL SPEAKING?
This app listens to your actual voice. After every question, you get:
• Level-based scoring aligned with B1, B2, and C1 descriptors
• Highlighted grammar errors with explanations
• Word-by-word pronunciation feedback
• Sample C1-level answers from GPT
• AI-generated follow-up questions

🎤 FULL MOCK TESTS
Take complete Multilevel Speaking mock tests — all parts of the exam (introduction, monologue with cue card, discussion) in one session. Realistic timing with the 1-minute preparation timer for the long-turn task. Mock results are saved to your history so you can track improvement.

📚 PRACTICE BY PART OR TOPIC
Drill specific weaknesses:
• Part 1.1 — Introduction questions
• Part 1.2 — Personal information / hometown / studies
• Part 2 — Long-turn cue card with prep timer
• Part 3 — Abstract discussion
Filter by topic (technology, environment, education, travel, society, and more).

📊 PROGRESS TRACKING
• Study streaks and weekly charts
• Score trends — see your level improve
• Detailed history with audio playback
• Weekly and monthly leaderboards
• Vocabulary list to grow your range

🔊 4 NATURAL EXAMINER VOICES
Practice with different accents:
• Sarah (US female) • Lily (UK female)
• Charlie (US male) • Roger (UK male)

💡 LEARN WITH TIPS & GUIDES
Built-in pages cover:
• Multilevel scoring criteria explained
• Pronunciation tips and drills
• Sample questions library

📱 WORKS ON YOUR PHONE OR INSIDE TELEGRAM
Multilevel Speaking runs as a native Android app and as a Telegram Mini App — your progress syncs across both.

🆓 FREE TO START
Try practice and mock tests on the free plan — no card required. Upgrade for higher daily limits.

📞 SUPPORT
Questions? Message @MultilevelSpeakingbot on Telegram or email hafizovshohbek@gmail.com.

Built for Uzbekistan's national Multilevel English exam takers. AI scoring is intended to help you practice and is not an official Multilevel exam result.
```
