Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Multilevel Speaking ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and protects user information when you use the Multilevel Speaking mobile app (Android) and the Telegram Mini App at multilevel.identify.uz.

1. Information We Collect

  • Telegram account info: When you sign in via Telegram, we receive your Telegram user ID, first name, username, and (after you tap "Share Contact" in the bot) your phone number. We do not receive your Telegram password.
  • Voice recordings: When you complete a speaking practice or mock test, the app records your microphone audio and uploads it to our servers for transcription and AI scoring. Recordings are deleted from our servers within 30 days of upload.
  • Practice results: Transcriptions, AI-generated CEFR/Multilevel scores, and feedback are stored against your account so you can review them in History.
  • Usage data: Session timestamps, number of mock tests / practice attempts taken, subscription status. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To transcribe your speech using Groq (Whisper Large V3) and to generate AI feedback using OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini). Audio and text are processed under each provider's terms; they do not use your data to train their models when accessed via API.
  • To show you your study history, progress charts, and leaderboard ranking.
  • To deliver notifications via Telegram bot.
  • To enforce subscription plan limits.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share only what is strictly necessary for the service:

  • Groq — for speech-to-text transcription of recordings.
  • OpenAI — for AI scoring and sample-answer generation. We send transcribed text, not raw audio.
  • Telegram — for authentication and bot notifications.
  • Hosting provider (Hetzner) — our servers are located in Europe.

4. Data Retention

  • Voice recordings: deleted within 30 days.
  • Transcriptions, scores, history: kept until you request deletion.
  • Account and contact info: kept while your account is active.

5. Your Rights

You can request to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your study history.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hafizovshohbek@gmail.com or message @MultilevelSpeakingbot.

6. Children's Privacy

Multilevel Speaking is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Security

All traffic uses HTTPS. Tokens are stored hashed. Voice recordings are stored on encrypted volumes. We follow industry-standard practices but no system is 100% secure — please notify us of any vulnerabilities.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced via the Telegram bot. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version.
